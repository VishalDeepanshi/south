About us

Southshore Innovations Private Limited is focused on bringing technology to the field of publishing and its distribution & logistics. With this as a core, we have looked at the traditional publishing market as it exists today, the number of small publishers, the fragmentation in the market in India and have tailored products/ solutions which would help this segment become more efficient and give them the ability to scale.

In today’s world, data or rather the ability to collect, maintain and retrieve and analyse data has become very important and this is another area of focus for us.

Having our roots in the publishing field, we love working with authors and help them self publish their content through our partnerships in the industry.

Online has become a very important channel and we sell books through this channel. This is done through aggregators like Amazon / Flipkart as well as our own sites. We offer this service to small and medium publishers across regions / languages.

Publishing

Self publishing as an area has grown very fast over the last few years in India and the options for authors to self publish have become many. A lot of this is driven by technology. At Southshore, we understand the effort it takes as an author to write out content for a book. We believe that each book is a piece of art and needs to be treated as such and given the attention it deserved. This is where we aim to make a difference in working with authors and partnering them through the journey of their book coming into this world. .

Southshore owns the publishing imprint ‘Inca” and this is the vehicle we use to publish authors who works with us.

If you are an author and are looking to publish, we would love to hear from you. Please click here to reach us.

At Southshore, we provide a range of services in the publishing area covering editorial services, typesetting, cover design to name a few.

In addition we also offer the service of typesetting of dissertations for aspiring scholars.

Please reach out to us for further information.

Products
Prime WMS

Prime WMS is a cloud based warehouse management system. The system covers all processes in a warehouse and manages the inventory and supply chain and distribution side of the business as well.

Prime WMS is SaaS based and hence the time required to set up and get operational is very minimal. We also help you in setting up and bringing your initial data upto date before going live.

Prime WMS is subscription based and hence the outflow in terms of cost to the organization / user is low. There are flexible options like a monthly / annual plan with options to switch between the same as required by the user.

Prime WMS is flexible and apart from warehouse management, also allows the organization / user to manage their direct , indirect and capes expenses on a day on day basis with a facility to port the same to their billing system.

Bukedge

At Southshore, our intent has been to use technology to help solve problems in specific areas. With this as the focus, “BukEdge” is our solution to the publishing industry at large. It is a secure cloud based SaaS product that covers different aspects of the publishing industry and has been built modularly. It covers

  • Author acquisition & contracting
  • File and print management
  • Sales & distribution
  • Royalty management

Being modular a customer has the option to choose which modules they would like to choose and use. For more information please refer the Bukedge site here

Booksales

Southshore innovations sells books through its brand “ BooksIndia”. We sell through aggregators like Amazon & Flipkart as well through our own websites booksindia.net and booksindia.store.

We have a strong fulfilment backbone with the ability to cater to customers across the country as well as tie-up’s with our print partners to print and dispatch a book within 48 hours in the event the book is not in our stock.
