About us

Southshore Innovations Private Limited, based out of Chennai is focused on different areas of the publishing field. At SouthShore, we love the world of books and the company’s ethos, and focus are in and around this area.

We constantly think about how we can look at content and the way it can be presented. With this as the focus, we work with authors and help them self-publish their books, we run an online bookstore supporting sales of books across genres as two of our core areas.

The publishing segment in India is fragmented and has a lot of small publishers who sometimes find it difficult to scale. Our intent is to help them get more efficient and with this in mind, we have used technology to provide solutions to solving several such problems faced by the small and medium players in the publishing industry.

Technology
Bukedge

At Southshore, our intent has been to use technology to help solve problems in specific areas. With this as the focus, “BukEdge” is our solution to the publishing industry at large. It is a secure cloud based SaaS product that covers different aspects of the publishing industry and has been built modularly. It covers

  • Author acquisition & contracting
  • File and print management
  • Warehousing
  • Sales & distribution
  • Royalty management

Being modular a customer has the option to choose which modules they would like to choose and use. For more information please refer the Bukedge site here

Custom development

Southshore works with a team of designers and developers and is constantly evolving and adapting to changes in the technology space and the new technologies that come in.

Our team has the functional experience across multiple domains and we work with our clients and understand requirements not just from a application / programming perspective but primarily from a customer / market need perspective. This ensures the application built is relevant.

We enjoy challenges and please let us know your requirements and we will reach you and discuss further.

Booksales

Southshore innovations sells books through its brand “ BooksIndia”. We sell through aggregators like Amazon & Flipkart as well through our own websites booksindia.net and booksindia.store.

We have a strong fulfilment backbone with the ability to cater to customers across the country as well as tie-up’s with our print partners to print and dispatch a book within 48 hours in the event the book is not in our stock.
